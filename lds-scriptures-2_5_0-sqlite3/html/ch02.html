The Printed Word and Imported Text

In converting the Scriptures from one format to another, there has been a few instances where I would have to decide how to format or include certain parts of the printed text.

The differences between the printed word and the imported text can be categorized in three areas: ommitted text, formatting styles and naming standards.

While some changes are necessary, it's worth noting that the actual text of the scriptures have themselves been unchanged, and in fact, every effort has been made to standardize everything based on the formatting and style of the printed works.
