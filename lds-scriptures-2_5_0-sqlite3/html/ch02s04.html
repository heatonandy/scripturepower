Naming Standards

When referencing the scriptures, one verse can have many titles. Do you use John 3:16, St. John 3:16, Saint John 3:16 or St John 3:16?

While you may have your own preference, these packages standardize on a format that the Church has provided in their standard works. I call it the ADFI, and it's used in all their footnotes and scripturual references.

Abbreviations and Designations in Footnotes and Index

If you look at the front of your Book of Mormon you'll see a page with that title. Instead of trying to dream up a naming scheme, the project uses the same as the standard works. So in the case of our example, the verse would be referenced as "John 3:16" in the indexes and footnotes.

In the rest of the documenation, I refer to this reference as the ADFI.
